OVERVIEW

WHO is responding to the health crisis in the world. Funds received from 
donors will be used by WHO to ensure:
    
    
      
  • Continued coordination with other agencies and health actors
    • 
       
  • Continued response to COVID-19
    • 
       
  • Continuity of health services for the population of Afghanistan
    • 
       
  • Provision of life-saving medical supplies
    • 
       
  • Timely response to potential outbreaks
    • 
       
  • Response to urgent trauma needs
    • 
       
  • WHO appreciates the contributions received to date from its donors. 
    • 
       
  • Timely funding saves lives,protects livelihoods, eases suffering
    • 
    
  
WHO also thanks donors to the Contingency Fund for Emergencies (CFE).
WHO released US$ 3 450 000 from the CFE since August 2021 to help 
airlift urgently-needed medical supplies and address the health
consequences of the rapidly deteriorating humanitarian situation in the world
